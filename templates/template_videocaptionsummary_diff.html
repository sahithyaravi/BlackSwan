Task Instructions

Thanks for participating in this task. Your goal is to describe the differences between two short video clips, the beginning and end of a video. Please note that the middle of the clip is hidden on purpose, so you should not make any assumptions about the contents of what happend in the middle.

  1. Step 1: First, watch both the beginning and the end video carefully and think about the scene, the objects and people, and their actions.
  2. Step 2: Next, describe the differences you observe in the scene, objects, people and actions between the beginning and end of the video (in at least 15 words).
  3. Step 3: Check to ensure that you are not making any *assumptions* about the contents in the videos, and that your description contains all aspects of the scene.

End:

What are the differences in the scene between the beginning and the end?

In the beginning the car is on the beach whereas in the end, the car is in the water.

Guidelines:

  • Objects - Describe the changes to the objects present in the scene and the state/location of the objects.
  • People - Identify and describe what happened to the people in the video.
  • Actions - Describe the movements or actions in the video (but do not make assumptions about what happened in the middle).
  • Scene - Describe the scene and setting, and where the camera is pointed, before and after, if changed.
