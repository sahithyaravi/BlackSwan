Task Instructions

Thanks for participating in this task. Your goal is to describe a very short video clip (can be a small part of a longer video) to the best of your ability.

  1. Step 1: First, watch the video carefully and think about the scene, the objects and people, and their actions.
  2. Step 2: Next, describe the video in a few sentences. You must write at least 25 words, but longer descriptions are welcome.
  3. Step 3: Check to ensure that you are not making any *assumptions* about the contents in the video, and that your description contains all aspects of the scene.

Example 1

Watch this clip and describe everything about the contents of the video:

A person is walking towards a body of water, may be a lake on a board walk with a fishing stick. Then, the person sees a silver sedan moving towards the water on his left. The person steps off the boardwalk towards the car hastily. The video is in the perspective of the person walking.

Example 2

Watch this clip and describe everything about the contents of the video:

A man walks down a small mud hill towards the camera. There appears to be an upside-down wheelchair to the right of the scene. The secene is set in a farm-like area, with a shed in the back and mostly grassy fields.

Example 3

Watch this clip and describe everything about the contents of the video:

Two kids are sitting in a cardboard box, and they have started going down the stairs of the house while riding in the box. The camera is pointed looking up towards the stairs.

Guidelines:

  • Objects - Describe the objects present in the scene and the state/location of the objects.
  • People - Identify and describe the people in the video (eg. young child, old lady...).
  • Actions - Describe the movements or actions in the video.
  • Scene - Descibe the scene and setting, and where the camera is pointed.
