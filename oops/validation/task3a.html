Task Instructions

Thanks for participating in this HIT! In this task, you will evaluate a description written from what happened in a video. Each video is divided into three parts Part 1, Part 2 and Part 3, described below.

  • Part 1: The beginning or setup of the scene in the video.
  • Part 2: The interesting event in the video.
  • Part 3: The result or outcome of the event in the video.
You will be provided with all three parts of the video. Your task is to validate an explanation about what happened in the video based on the following criteria:

Criteria:

  1. Correctness (5 Point Scale) Check whether the answer correctly describes what could have happened in Part 2 based on the entire video.
  2. Depth and Thoughtfulness (5 Point Scale) How much depth in reasoning is covered in the description? Is there sufficient reasoning to support the description?
  3. Level of Detail (5 Point Scale) Does the description have sufficient level of detail to easily identify and differentiate between people/objects and actions?
  4. Grammar (Yes/No) Are there any major grammatical errors or issues?
  5. Visual Contradiction (Yes/No) Does the description follow the content in the video, or contradict the contents of the video?
